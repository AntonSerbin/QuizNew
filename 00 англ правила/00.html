Rules of the game.


This Quiz can be played with one or more teams. Recomended two teams and bigger.

The recommended number of participants in the team is 3-4 people. It is also necessary to choose a responsable person who will switch tasks and check answers. This person can play all tasks as usual players.

After the teams are formed, each team will need a form for answers (a regular piece of paper will be enough).

Before the start of each level, there will be local rules that explain what players need to do.

After the end of the level, the responsible person collects the forms with the answers and checks the number of correct answers.

Spelling errors and inaccuracies of answers are allowed. The difference in the format of answers is not allowed (for example, at the level there was a ANSWER FORMAT - AUTHOR'S NAME, and the players indicated the NAME of the work in the form).

For each correct answer, the team gets one ball.

At the end of each level, the responsible person records and announces the current results of the game.

The winner is the team that scores more points for all levels.
